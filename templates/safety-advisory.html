Zoom Jet Packs Safety Advisory Document

This document serves to provide important safety advice to the leasee of a new jet pack from Zoom Jet Packs. By signing this document, you acknowledge that you have been advised of these safety points and agree to adhere to them.

Safety Dos:

  1. Always wear suitable protective clothing: This includes a helmet, gloves, and fire-resistant suit.
  2. Only fly if you have a license: Ensure you have a valid jet pack operator's license.
  3. Attend a safety training course: Before operating the jet pack, attend a safety training course provided by a certified institution.
  4. Perform a pre-flight check: Always check the jet pack for any visible damage or leaks before each flight.
  5. Fly in clear weather: Avoid flying in adverse weather conditions such as rain, fog, or high winds.
  6. Maintain a safe altitude: Always fly at a safe altitude to avoid collisions with buildings and other structures.
  7. Keep a safe distance from people and animals: Maintain a minimum distance of 30 feet from people and animals.
  8. Follow traffic rules: Adhere to all air traffic rules and regulations.
  9. Use safety equipment: Always use the safety harness and other safety equipment provided.
  10. Report any issues immediately: If you notice any issues with the jet pack, report them to Zoom Jet Packs immediately.

Safety Don'ts:

  1. Don't do stunts: Performing stunts can lead to accidents and damage to the jet pack.
  2. Don't operate under the influence: Never operate the jet pack under the influence of alcohol or drugs.
  3. Don't fly near airports: Keep a safe distance from airports and other restricted airspace.
  4. Don't exceed the weight limit: Never exceed the maximum weight limit specified for the jet pack.
  5. Don't ignore warning signals: If any warning lights or signals activate during flight, land immediately and report the issue.
  6. Don't fly over crowds: Avoid flying directly over crowded areas.
  7. Don't ignore maintenance schedules: Regular maintenance is crucial for safe operation. Follow the maintenance schedule provided.
  8. Don't modify the jet pack: Any modifications to the jet pack must be approved by Zoom Jet Packs.
  9. Don't leave the jet pack unattended: Always secure the jet pack when not in use.
  10. Don't fly in restricted areas: Always check for and respect local and national restrictions on jet pack use.
